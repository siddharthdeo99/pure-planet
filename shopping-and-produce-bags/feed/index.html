We Must Try To Reduce Our Impact

  • The average amount of rubbish created by an Australian produces approximately 1500 kilograms per year.
  • Australians discard around 25 million tonnes of rubbish every year, with a great cost to the environment.
  • The amount of plastic Australians use each year is enough to cover an area the size of Queensland.
  • The average Australian throws away more than 200kgs of food every year, which equates to around $1500 each time they visit the supermarket.

A more sustainable lifestyle can benefit both people and the planet alike. It’s never too late to make some changes in order to live greener. In fact, making even little tweaks here or there can be enough to start living sustainably without much effort at all!

The first step is finding out what works best for you and your family. The more you recycle and reuse things in your daily life, the less waste is generated. Just a few simple swaps could make a big difference! The good news is that it doesn’t take much to make eco-friendly changes in everyday life!
