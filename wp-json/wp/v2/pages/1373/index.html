Our tape is made from renewable materials, so you’ll never have to worry about running out. It’s certified to break down in a commercial compost facility within 60 days and decompose with zero toxicity for worms and microorganisms.

The Ecotape is designed to decompose at an appropriate rate, no matter what setting its placed into.
