Return Policy<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tOur policy lasts 21 days. In order for us to process the return of any items, a return request must have been lodged within 21 days from the date of purchase.

\n\nItems must be returned within 21 days from the date of purchase, in new and unused condition with all original packaging and labels\/tags still attached.

\n\nItems must be unaltered, undamaged and unwashed, while in the customer\u2019s possession.

\n\nA Refund Request must be lodged within 21 days of purchase to be eligiable.

\n\nPrior to you returning your item, we will require you to fill out our Refund Request form.

\n\nIf your order is eligible for refund, we will require you to return your item to us at your own cost. Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

\n\nIf you are approved, then your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment, within a certain amount of days, less the postage costs incurred by us for your item. Postage costs are currently a flat rate of $7.95.

\n\nLate or missing refunds (if applicable)

\n\nIf you haven\u2019t received a refund yet, first check your bank account again.

\n\nThen contact your credit card company, it may take some time before your refund is officially posted.

\n\nNext contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted.

\n\nIf you\u2019ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at hello@ecoessentials.com.au

\n\nExchanges (if applicable)

\n\nWe only replace items if they are defective or damaged.\u00a0 If you need to exchange it for the same item, send us an email at hello@ecoessentials.com.au

\n\nShipping

\n\nTo return your product, you should mail your product to:

\n\nPure Planet

\n\n2.08, T2A, Warawara Cct,

\n\nQuakers Hill

\n\nNSW 2763

\n\nYou will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of shipping will be deducted from your refund.

\n\nWe recommend you consider using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance.
