Why EcoEssentials was created<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

The EcoEssentials Store is a sustainable store that sells products for the environmentally-conscious consumer. We sell products designed to reduce your carbon footprint and live an eco-friendly lifestyle. If you want to start living with less impact on the environment, then you have come to the right place!<\/p>

We offer a variety of products, from reusable grocery bags to reusable drinking straws. We believe that sustainable living begins with small steps and simple changes in behaviour-and what better way than by making small changes to your lifestyle?<\/p>

Pure Planet was created from the dream of a one-stop-shop for everyone to find a replacement for single-use everyday items! The idea for sustainable living is often overwhelming, but we want to make it easier! We hope you will support our small business and our philosophy of sustainability.<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

We want to help you make a difference!<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\tMany people are unaware of the environmental impact that their lifestyle choices can have. By making some simple changes, you can reduce your carbon footprint and live a more eco-friendly life. The first step to taking control is to figure out where you want to start! We want to help you discover ways you can reduce your impact on the environment.

\nA more sustainable lifestyle can benefit both people and the planet alike. It’s never too late to make some changes in order to live greener. In fact, making even little tweaks here or there can be enough to start living sustainably without much effort at all! The first step is finding out what works best for you and your family. The more you recycle and reuse things in your daily life, the less waste is generated. Just a few simple swaps could make a big difference! The good news is that it doesn’t take much to make eco-friendly changes in everyday life!\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t

We care about the environment<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

We care about sustainable living. We also offer sustainable products, instead of using cheaper synthetic fibres that come with a heavy environmental toll on natural resources and people’s health. Nearly all of our packing is designed to be environmentally friendly and reusable too! And EcoEssentials Store cares more than anything else in the world for you – so we take care of not only your well-being but also your planet as well!<\/p>

All of our products are vegan and animal friendly. You won’t find any animal products in our range of sustainable options. Every product we sell is also designed to be as environmentally friendly as possible, so you can rest assured knowing that your purchase has the least impact on the planet.<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

    it Can Be Easy to make a few small changes<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
    \n\t\t\t\t
    \n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

    Sustainable living is for everyone. You can be sustainable without being inconvenienced! Some people might enjoy a more eco-friendly lifestyle while others may find that they prefer to have the conveniences of modern life, such as disposable coffee cups or plastic utensils. However, we are all responsible for our own impact on the environment. Therefore we believe a few small changes can have a big impact on the environment.\u00a0<\/p>

    It’s important to define your own goals so you know what will work best in order to live sustainably. Talk to your family or housemates and discuss how together you could make a big difference.
